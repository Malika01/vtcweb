2-CREDIT COURSE

Course name: VENTURE. EXPLORE. BACKPACK. (EXC1157)

We are not just about organizing treks, but we also spread awareness that pertains to such treks. Our club members are known for their elaborate display of zest. Don't believe us? Check it out for yourself! Learn the proper way to climb, how to tie different types of knots, first aid, navigation techniques(without gps, of course! ) and much more. Throughout the course you'll receive first-hand updates on official treks and our events and much more. The best part? You earn 2 credits! Join us this Winter semester for an experience you won't regret.
